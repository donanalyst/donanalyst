SuperStore Sales Analysis Dashboard

OBJECTIVES:

There are a lot of business questions that can be answered through from this dashboard. This cover a variety of key performance indicator (KPI's) that can help the business gain valuable insights into their sales performance, and overall business health. List down below are the possible business questions:

  • What are the overall sales trends? Are sales increasing or decreasing?
  • How do current sales compare to the previous year?
  • What are the total sales by category and sub-category?
  • Which products are the top sellers?
  • Which products are underperforming?

  • How many orders are placed by each customer?
