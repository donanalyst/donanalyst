Before diving into the world of data, I gained valuable experience working in a BPO company, specifically in the Tech Support Department. In this role, I developed strong skills in communication, problem-solving, critical thinking, and handling different kinds of cases. I began to understand the world of business and how to keep the reputation not just of the company but also of the brand that we provided support to our clients and their customers.

My passion for uncovering insights and driving data-driven decisions started while I was working there, with my headphones on, talking to many customers. This was one of the reasons that led me to pursue a career in data analytics. Back in 2022, as I was about to leave the office one day, I happened to pass by a friend who was working as a Real-Time Analyst. Observing his work piqued my curiosity about how he handled data so smoothly from the beginning to the end of our shifts. Seeing him working in Excel further fueled my interest, and I decided to learn more about it.

To hone my skills, I enrolled in various online courses on platforms like Coursera and Udemy, including Google Data Analytics, The Ultimate Microsoft Excel Mastery Courses by Udemy and Data Visualization with Power BI by Coursera. Through consistent dedication and motivation, I successfully passed the Microsoft Power BI Certification exam on July 11, 2024.

Now, I'm excited to apply my expertise to help your businesses and your organizations achieve your data-driven goals, whether it's optimizing the operations, improving your customer understanding, or to be a help in making better strategic decisions.
